Should we build a border wall?

The promise fueled an unprecedented election: We will build a wall. The questions launched an unprecedented journey: Could it be built? Would it work? The USA TODAY NETWORK looked at every foot of a 2,000-mile border. Now, you can too. You'll meet remarkable people and discover the border's untold stories. Is a border wall worth the consequences? The issue is vast and complex. But the stories that follow are designed to help you understand. No matter what you already think about a border wall, you'll learn something new here. The exploration is yours to begin.
